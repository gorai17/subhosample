Our Work
1. Inspection of materials before erection of any machinery.
2. Foundation of lifting equipment such as Tower Crane, Passenger cum
Material Hoist, Builder Hoist, Suspended Platform and Mast climbing platform.
3. Erection and dismantling of all above lifting equipment.
4. Troubleshooting of all lifting equipment.
5. Manual dismantling with different methods by providing approved drawing
of the whole operation to be done along with job specific method statement, lifting plan and other required documents.
6. Provide on field and official training with PPT to all the staffs related to Tower Crane safe operation and maintenance.
7. We take AMC (annual maintenance contract) for all above machineries by deploying experienced operator, Signalman and technician for safe and sound operation. By doing on time maintenance we use to increase the life of all major and minor spare parts of the machine.
8. We repair all electronic items such as VFD, PLC, Electronic cards etc.
9. Programming of PLC, VFD of Tower Crane, Passenger Hoist and other machine.
10. We supply experienced operators, helpers, signalman and engineers.
11. We provide consultation to choose proper location and perfect model of machine for particular application.
12. We provide working methodology along with drawing for anchorage, overlapping etc.
13. We supply spares parts for all brands of tower crane, passenger hoist and builder hoist
